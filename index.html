Julia Readclose

Julia is a senior and an enthusiastic leader who is so delighted to have your support in making this organization possible! She enjoys making a variety of crafts (felt plushies, origami kusudamas, nylon flowers, and more!) as well as working with children, from tutoring them in academic subjects to teaching them how to swim. At school, Julia loves studying computer science and chemistry. She also swims year-round in DACA's National Development team and is the Team Captain of her high school swim team after having been a member of the Varsity swim team for four years. In addition, she loves drawing and animation and hopes to one day combine her interests in art, science, and engineering.
